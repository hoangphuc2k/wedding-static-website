Attire Guide

Dress Code

Join us in creating a beautiful celebration with appropriate attire for each special event across our two days of festivities.

Church Ceremony

Formal, conservative attire for our sacred ceremony. Gentlemen should wear suits or dress shirts with dress pants. Ladies should choose dresses or suits, avoiding short or strapless styles.

Wedding Reception

Cocktail attire for our elegant evening celebration. Gentlemen may wear suits or blazers. Ladies may choose cocktail dresses or sophisticated jumpsuits.

Vu Quy Ceremony

Traditional Vietnamese attire or formal wear in vibrant colors that symbolize luck and happiness in Vietnamese culture. Embrace the beauty of our heritage.

Thanh Hon Ceremony

Smart casual attire for our intimate family ceremony. Gentlemen may wear dress shirts without ties with khakis. Ladies may choose maxi dresses or elegant casual wear.
